1. Project Summary

We need a first batch of Meta ad creatives for MedVirtual. The goal is to test a few clear angles around hiring full-time virtual medical and dental staff through MedVirtual — not building an endless copy library.

This is a modest first Meta test budget. Keep the batch small, specific, and production-ready.

2. Messaging Rules

Use

  • MedVirtual (brand name only — never MedVirtual.ai in ad copy)
  • full-time virtual staff
  • virtual staff member
  • part of your practice team
  • hire through MedVirtual
  • trained virtual medical staff
  • virtual medical admin
  • virtual dental admin
  • virtual medical biller
  • starting at $10/hour (where relevant)

Avoid

  • MedVirtual.ai
  • managed service
  • outsourced front desk
  • front desk replacement
  • we handle your front desk
  • MedVirtual becomes your front desk
  • vague hype
  • overly long captions
  • implying MedVirtual is the clinic’s front desk
  • positioning MedVirtual as a managed service provider

Correct positioning

  • Clinics and practices hire full-time virtual staff through MedVirtual.
  • The virtual staff member becomes part of their practice team.
  • The staff member works remotely but supports the clinic’s daily workflow.
  • Show the virtual staff member handling calls, scheduling, and admin work.
  • Use specific use-case copy — not only high-level brand messaging.

5. Designer / VA Notes

  • On-image text = short. Facebook primary text carries the longer explanation.
  • Show the virtual staff member doing the work — headset, scheduling screen, insurance review.
  • Staff should feel like part of the clinic team — not an external call center.
  • Use clean healthcare / clinic visuals. No tiny text. Do not overcrowd the creative.
  • Brand name: MedVirtual only — never MedVirtual.ai in ad copy or on-image text.
  • CTA on creative: Book a demo.
  • Site-backed price claim only: Starting at $10/hour (optional stamp).
