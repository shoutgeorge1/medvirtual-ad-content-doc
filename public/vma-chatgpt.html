ChatGPT Workflow

Step 1

Upload the approved examples

Upload one or more approved 1:1 masters so ChatGPT can see the MedVirtual baseline.

Step 2

Tell ChatGPT to analyze only

Ask for hierarchy, contrast, and role clarity notes — do not invent claims.

Step 3

Request original concepts

Ask for 15–20 original concepts in the approved direct-response style. Explicitly say NO PINK and do not copy competitors.

Step 4

Select one direction

Pick the strongest concepts for design or AI plate testing.

Step 5

Confirm exact copy

Lock headline, benefits, offer, and CTA before final art.

Step 6

Generate or brief the designer

AI-generated ads may be used for testing. Designers may also build from the brief directly.

Step 7

Check every word

Verify spelling, price, services, Spanish, badges, logos, hands, and accidental pink.

Step 8

Build required aspect ratios

Rebuild approved concepts for 1:1, 4:5, 9:16, and 1.91:1 — not a crop.

CHATGPT MAY GENERATE COMPLETE ADS. The team may use AI-generated creative for testing.
